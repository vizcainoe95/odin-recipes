Tacos de Arrachera

Finished product

Ingredients

  • Tortillas de maiz
  • Arrachera (trimmed and tenderized)
  • Onion
  • Cilantro
  • Lime
  • Goya
  • Mexican lager
  • Salt & Pepper

Steps

  1. Marinate Arrachera in beer, lime, Goya, salt, and pepper.
  2. Let marinate for 2-8 hours
  3. Cook meat on grill to preferred wellness
  4. While meat is cooking, finely chop onion and cilantro and set aside
  5. Heat up tortillas on grill or in microwave
  6. Assemble tacos by starting with tortillas, placing meat, and adding cilantro and onion
  7. (Optional) Add salsa if available
